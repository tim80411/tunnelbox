Everything you need to share local sites: Cloudflare Tunnel, reverse proxy, CLI, Local HTTPS, LAN sharing, share-group presets, frp self-hosted tunneling, custom domains — and auto-updates, always. Free is great for single-demo use and everyday development.

Free mode supports 2 simultaneous shares. When you start a third, TunnelBox shows a dialog: stop one of the existing shares to continue, or upgrade to Pro for unlimited concurrent shares. No data is lost; your share configs are preserved.

On Free, closing the TunnelBox window stops the app and all active shares — TunnelBox is a one-at-a-time demo tool. Pro mode hides to the system tray when you close the window, keeping tunnels alive as long as you like. You can also enable "Launch at startup" for permanent hosting.
